Privacy Policy

Last updated: May, 2019

I-Tourist is I-Tourist Application (the 'App'). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the App.

We use your Personal Information only for providing services and improving the App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Multiplatform mobile and web applications will serve tourists and diplomats and service providers in one place and the model is B2B2C.

Tourist / Diplomats will download I-Tourist mobile application from mobile market, or will use web pages to choose following services:

  • Sim card charges (for those who purchased sim card)
  • Consular services (interactive form filling and submission)
  • Check-Up services (medical checkup schedule and status)
  • Insurance services (health, baggage)
  • On-Line Doctor (consultations, remote observation, schedule meeting, electronic prescriptions)
  • Short time Apartments rent services
  • Discounts and Benefits
  • Trips
  • Tickets for show, performances, concerts, museums and others

Each of one of these services will charge payment resource stored in application (Credit Card, PayPal or Device mobile account settings-InApp Purchase).

Each purchase of user is written in back office system. The I-Tourist business have or will have relevant agreements with service providers, so after each purchase of user the I-Tourist back office system will sent payment to service provider based on agreement. Could be scheduled once in period (hour, day and month) or real time.

Service providers, such as insurance, consular services, doctors will use web Multiplatform application to login (two factor authentications, accepting and signing NDA) and serve users requests.

When application is first running user is escorted during initial setup. On first screens user asked to configure payment method, color palette, application permissions. Next user routed to application functional menus and options and through this process learning how to access each option in application.
